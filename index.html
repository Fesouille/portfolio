About me

I am a junior web developer with lots of motivation and talent. I'm interested in all kinds of web development, but my prefered fields are algorithms and back-end developement (Javascript, PHP, databases,...). I also have skills and experience in other fields like project management and business consulting.

In 2015, I graduated as Business Engineer with a master degree from the Solvay Brussels School of Economics and Management and the Saint-Louis University (Belgium).

After graduating, I worked two years as SAP Consultant for Deloitte Consulting in Brussels. Though this was a very enriching profesionnal experience, I realised that I enjoy much more being on the developement side than the business side. As a result, I re-oriented myself to the web developement and here I am, gaining a bit more of experience every day. I can say that I enjoy my life.

In addition to my technical skills, my most developed soft skills are my problem solving & debugging capabilities, my strong team spirit, my ability to learn efficiently & fast and my particular attention to details.
Do not hesitate to challenge me.
